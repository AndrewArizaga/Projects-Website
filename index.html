About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), currently pursuing a B.Eng with minors in Computer Science and Mathematics. My professional experience includes working as an Energy Management Engineering Intern at the City of Brampton, where I contributed to optimizing energy usage and integrating renewable energy solutions. Previously, I served as a Hardware Engineering Intern at Cence Power, focusing on the design and testing of power electronics and embedded systems.

My skill set spans PCB design (using Altium and KiCAD), embedded systems, digital systems, and energy systems. I am proficient in programming languages such as Python, C++, and MATLAB, and have a strong foundation in AutoCAD Electrical for system design. Additionally, I have hands-on experience with microcontrollers, power management systems, and hardware-software integration.

Relevant coursework includes Digital Systems Design, Embedded Systems, and Electric Networks, with a focus on practical applications in robotics, control systems, and renewable energy technologies.
