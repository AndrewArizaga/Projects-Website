About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew Arizaga, an aspiring Electrical Engineer with a passion for solving real-world challenges through innovative design and technology. I am currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), complemented by minors in Computer Science and Mathematics.

My journey has been fueled by curiosity and dedication. I have experience working on diverse projects, including:

  • Energy Management Intern at the City of Brampton, optimizing energy usage for sustainability.
  • Hardware Engineering Intern at Cence Power, contributing to hardware integration and system reliability.
  • TMSR Solar Car Team Lead, developing battery management systems and power electronics.
  • Project Manager for TMBR Baja Racing, leading vehicle electrical system designs.

My certifications, including SOLIDWORKS Professional and Additive Manufacturing Associate, highlight my dedication to mastering technical tools and CAD design.

Beyond technical work, I volunteer with the Ontario Society of Professional Engineers (OSPE) to promote innovation and community outreach in engineering.
